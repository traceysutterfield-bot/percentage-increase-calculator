What This Calculator Does

This fraction to percentage calculator converts a fraction into its percent equivalent. It divides the numerator by the denominator to get a decimal, then multiplies by 100.

It’s useful for schoolwork, test prep, statistics, and everyday situations where you need to interpret fractions as percentages.
