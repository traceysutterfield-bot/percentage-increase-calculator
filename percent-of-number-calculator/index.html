What This Calculator Does

This percent of a number calculator helps you find the value of a percentage applied to a number. It answers questions written as “X% of Y” (for example, 15% of 80).

It’s useful for everyday math like discounts, tips, tax, commissions, and splitting costs when you need a quick and accurate percentage result.

When Would You Use This Calculator?

  • Calculating a discount amount (like 25% off a price)
  • Finding a tip amount (like 18% of a restaurant bill)
  • Estimating tax or fees as a percent of a total
  • Calculating commission, profit margin portions, or allocations
  • Quickly checking percentage-based math without doing it by hand
