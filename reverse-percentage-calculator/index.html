What This Calculator Does

This reverse percentage calculator helps you find the original number when you only know a percentage and a resulting value. It’s the “work backwards” version of a percent-of-number calculation.

Use it to solve problems like “30 is 20% of what?” or to find an original price before a discount or increase.

When Would You Use This Calculator?

  • Finding the total when you know a part and its percentage
  • Calculating the original price before a percentage discount
  • Calculating the original value before a percentage increase
  • Reversing commissions, taxes, or percentage-based adjustments
  • Checking percentage math for bills, invoices, or reports

Common Questions This Calculator Can Answer

  • 30 is 20% of what number?
  • If the final price is 80 after a 20% discount, what was the original price?
  • If a value increased 15% and is now 230, what was it before?
  • How do I reverse a percentage calculation?
  • What is the reverse percentage formula?
